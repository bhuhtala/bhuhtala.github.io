About Me

Let Me Introduce Myself!

My name is Delilah and I live in Waco, Texas with my dog Benji.

Cooking has been my passion for almost a decade, and it all started when I was done being afraid to get crafty in the kitchen. I was the person who could barely boil water without something going wrong, so if I can do it, you can too!

My Fall Favorites

Three of my favorite fall recipes to make are:

  • Butternut Squash Soup
  • Pumpkin Alfredo Pasta
  • Maple Apple Blondies

What Else Besides Cooking?

We all know there is much more to life than food... although no experience seems complete without it! Benji and I love to get outside and enjoy the weather as much as we can. We'll toss a frisbee around, go for walks, and even do yoga in the park!
